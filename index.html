Our Story

La Forchetta began as a countryside dream—an ode to Sunday tables, shared bowls of pasta, and honest ingredients. We partner with small farms and mills to bring heritage grains, seasonal vegetables, and sustainable meats to your plate.

Every dough is naturally fermented. Every pasta is rolled by hand. Every plate is a quiet conversation between tradition and innovation.
